What we import

Premium fresh produce, sourced with care.

From plantation to port, we hand-pick every crate. Explore our six core categories — each backed by trusted growers and modern cold-chain logistics.

🍌

Bananas

Cavendish bananas selected at optimal ripeness, packed for long-haul freshness.

  • Origin: Ecuador, Philippines
  • Cartons of 18.14 kg
  • Year-round availability
🧅

Onions

Yellow, red and white onions in multiple sizes — firm, dry and well-cured.

  • Origin: Egypt, Netherlands, India
  • Mesh bags 5–25 kg
  • Long shelf life
🍈

Cantaloupes

Sweet, aromatic melons harvested at peak Brix and shipped under controlled temperature.

  • Origin: Spain, Morocco, Brazil
  • 5–8 fruits per carton
  • Seasonal availability
🫑

Peppers

Bell peppers in red, yellow and green — glossy, thick-walled and uniformly graded.

  • Origin: Netherlands, Spain, Turkey
  • 5 kg cartons
  • Mixed-color options
🍇

Grapes

Seedless table grapes — crisp, sweet and delivered with intact bloom.

  • Origin: Egypt, South Africa, Peru
  • 4.5 kg cartons or punnets
  • Multiple varieties
🍉

Watermelons

Seedless and seeded watermelons with deep red flesh and high sugar content.

  • Origin: Spain, Greece, Turkey
  • Bulk bins or cartons
  • Spring–summer season
